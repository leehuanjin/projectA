Applications open for Year 2017

Terms and Conditions

  1. Only NEW students who have formally accepted their offer to study full time in Swinburne are eligible to apply.
  2. Please email housing@swinburne.edu.my
    1. Your Name
    2. Your Swinburne Student ID
    3. RM50.00 non-refundable processing fee bank in slip for Swinburne Accomodation Application login access right.
      Click here for mode of payment details
  3. You will receive your login access right via e-mail within 3 working days upon completion of Step 2.
  4. Allocation of rooms is strictly on first-come-first-served basis and subject to availability
  5. Accommodation is not guaranteed for the duration of your course. Please check our office for details.
  6. FULL rental fee are to be paid before check in to the allocation room.
  7. Students, who has accepted the offer and later decided to cancel, will be subjected to a cancellation fee.
  8. Students who breach Accommodation Rules & Regulations and Tenancy Agreement may have their stay terminated.
  9. Students who has disciplinary records or outstanding fee will not eligible to apply.
  10. Read and understand Swinburne Housing Rules and Regulations.
