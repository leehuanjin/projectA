New Applicant

If you are planning to live in campus accomodation for the first time, these application guide may come in handy.

Application Guide

    1. Only NEW students who have formally accepted their offer to study full time in Swinburne are eligible to apply.
    2. Click the APPLY NOW button below to register and apply for the hostel.
      Take note that you will need to pay an upfront of RM500 booking fee to be able to complete the booking.
      Apply Now
    3. For more info, please email housing@Swinburne.edu.my

Your Accommodation Options

Twin sharing with fan

Rooms  :  Bed with mattress, Study table with shelf and chair, wardrobe, fan, blinds or curtain, mirror, lighting
Toilet & Bath  / Laundry   : Common / Laundry facilities (coin-operated washing machine & dryer)
Kitchen   :  Kitchen/ dining area equipped with refrigerator, gas stove and microwave oven
Security   :  24 hours security
Housekeeping services  :  For common area only
Water : Hot and cold water dispenser
Internet Access  :  Free wireless internet access

Rental  Rate Twin sharing with fan RM380 per person per month
Payment of hostel fee is to be paid per semester. Tenureship of the hostel room will be based on the students’ program duration. A booking fee of RM500.00 is required and must be included when the Accommodations Booking Form is submitted to the University. This shall be considered as partial hostel rental
